Our Standards

Netra Academic provides a trustworthy, high-quality academic publishing environment built on originality, rigorous reasoning, and respectful discourse. All submissions are reviewed in line with our editorial principles and academic standards. Articles must be original and authored by the submitter. Plagiarism, undisclosed AI-generated content, promotional material, and any form of offensive or discriminatory language are not permitted. Submissions should follow fundamental academic standards, including clear structure, logical flow, and appropriate citation of sources. Content that contributes meaningful analysis or scholarly value is prioritized. Netra Academic reserves the right to review, request revisions, edit, or remove content to ensure consistency, credibility, and academic integrity across the platform.
